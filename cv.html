//------------ Description

We offer mobile exhibition concept that consists of 4/6 immersive and interactive installations.
The past> Flux Island Exhibition at Vuotalo Next> Material Light Art Festival 1.2021
Near Future> Kotka Light Art Festival / confirmed.
The works:
Havanto - Interactive installation the abstract cell-like entities react to their surroundings by adapting their form and reflecting the environment.
Chikramane & enue - HAP encoded interactive video that can be participated by the audience in surprising manner.
Soroush, Sanchez,enue, Lee interactive and immersive installation. Its physical structures invite the visitor to be synchronized with the audience, then transforming into movement, sound and light poetry. The motor and material structures will be renegotiated in an open dialogue. Tiainen, EPHYRA is a projected artificial creature based on algorithm and interaction between the observer. Pre-recorded soundscape creates a strong rising tension between the sound and visuals.

//------------ Workshops

We are professional educators and lectures on new media content and interactive digital art. We provide workshops for all ages under the
topics of DIY electronics, creative coding and generative audiovisual art inter alia. We are open to negotiate the final content with festival organisers. The workshops were and are part of the concept in Vuotalo and Kotka Light Festival.
